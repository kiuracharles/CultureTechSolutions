    The director's take

    Peterson Miriichi, Director

    "Our main goal is:

    Empowering Communities: Our Commitment to Social Responsibility Our commitment to social responsibility extends to various initiatives aimed at supporting the less privileged and empowering the next generation. Education is a fundamental human right, and we are passionate about providing opportunities for learning and growth. That's why we are thrilled to announce our annual scholarship program for two secondary students. Additionally, our commitment to nurturing young talent extends to offering attachment and internship opportunities to two students each year, providing them with practical experience and mentorship to kickstart their careers. As part of our social corporate responsibility, we have committed to regularly visiting at least one children's home every quarter, bringing joy and care to those in need. These visits are not just about material aid; they are about creating moments of happiness and connection for children who deserve a brighter tomorrow. We offer assistance with travel arrangements, including visa bookings, hotel reservations, and passport applications, to facilitate smoother journeys for our customers. Furthermore, our business offers consultancy services, such as company registration, tax advisory, financial statements, and audit, are designed to support the growth and success of small and medium enterprises, contributing to the economic development of our communities. We invite our customers to join us in our mission to make a difference. When you choose Culture Tech Solutions as your preferred provider of computers, laptops, phone accessories, and business consultancy services, you are not just investing in top-quality products and expert advice – you are also contributing to these meaningful efforts to uplift and empower our communities. Together, let's build a brighter future, one where everyone ahs the opportunity to thrive.
    Thank you for being a part of our journey and for choosing to make a difference with Culture Tech Solutions. "
